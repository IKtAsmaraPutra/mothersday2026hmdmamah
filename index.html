Dear Mom,

There are moments in life when I realise, suddenly and completely, how lucky I am to have you. It happens in ordinary places: watching people on the roads to class, hearing a faimiliar voice, catching a familiar gesture that I somehow now find in myself.

You gave me more than a home. You gave me a sense of security, the courage to try, and the knowledge that there is someone i can rely on.

No gift today will ever match what you have given me in my life. But I hope this small moment makes you feel, even briefly, how deeply you are adored.

With all my love ♡
